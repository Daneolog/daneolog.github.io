The solution

Comprehensive SaaS (Software as a Service) solution to improve the fan experience with a new state-of-the-art stadium.

For example, the entry experience. We will provide a device that could improve the speed and quality of stadium ingress, allowing greeters to shift their focus from scanning tickets to welcoming fans, as well as capture data to inform future developments, like a fan loyalty program. To address this need, we will develop a self-service ticket reading device that is flexible, mobile, wireless, and battery operated, as well as a robust backend web and database infrastructure with the ability to communicate in real time with existing ticketing services, such as Ticketmaster.
